My personal interests

One of my main passions right now is snowboarding. I'm always looking for new ways to push my limits on the mountain. I love the adrenaline I get whenever I land a new trick or am one of the first people on the mountain that day to get the freshest powder.

I also love music, whether it's listening or playing. I've played the piano on and off my whole life and I've recently begun learning the bass guitar. Lastly, my most memorable experiences come from my travels. I have many travel plans for the future and hope one day to live abroad to explore more of the world. The places I have been include: Spain, England, Peru, Mexico, Canada, and all over the west and east coast of the US.
